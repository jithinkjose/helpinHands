Our Services

we are always available to provide care for your parent's various needs such as, health care, companionship, transportation, day outing, eldercare products and technical supports.
Health care

Our team fixes doctor appointments, takes your parents for doctor's visit, arranging bystanders, get the medicines, maintain medical reports, treatment planning, sending reminders and follow-up with them. Allied Health staff such as Dieticians, Physiotherapists & Psychologists are available to come home for consultations and therapy, when advised.

Palliative care

Once the patient is discharged from the hospital, our specially trained caregivers transfer the patient home and follow the defined care plan created by the doctors

Companion care

Your parents may sometimes feel lonely and need someone to talk our team can read or arrange books/magazines to your parents and listen to them

Transportation care

We provide cab for your parents for travel from one point to another or provide driver to help them with their commute

Leisure care

We organise family events so that your parents can able to spend time with family, ,arranging picnic to free your parents from tediousness , and arrange surprise events for your parents whether its their birthday or anniversary

Technical support care

We provide technical support and training to help older adults feel more comfortable with phones, computers, tablets, and more

Elder care products

We assist for buy various eldercare products and aids that will ease their daily life activities.

Insurance assistance

We assist for taking life insurance, health insurance, car insurance, home insurance, property management assistance ,health & travel insurance services.
